Day 1 - Open Space

This day will be self organized. There is no pre-set agenda and no pre-determined speakers. A simple scaffolding of time and space will be provided and YOU will decide what we talk about. We will use Open Space Technology to organize the day.

Open Space has four principles or rules and one law.

The four principles are:

  • Whoever shows up is the right group
  • Whatever happens is the only thing that could have
  • Whenever it starts is the right time
  • When it's over, it's over

And the Law of Two Feet:

If, during the course of the gathering, any person finds themselves in a situation where they are neither learning nor contributing, they must use their feet and go to some more productive place.

Day 2 - Coderetreat

Coderetreat is a day-long, intensive practice event, focusing on the fundamentals of software development and design.

Youʼll get the opportunity to take part in focused practice, away from the pressures of 'getting things done'. The coderetreat format has proven itself to be a highly effective means of skill improvement for software developers. Practicing the basic principles of modular and object-oriented design, you can improve your ability to write code that minimizes the cost of change over time.

What to expect from Coderetreat:

  • Bring your laptop, prepared with the development tools you want to use.
  • Be prepared to pair program.
  • Be prepared to test drive.
  • The goal of the event is not to solve the problem, it's to improve your skills.
